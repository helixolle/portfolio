About
olle lomberg davegård

I'm an interaction designer recently graduated from Linnaeus University, with a background that runs through 3D graphics, VFX, and digital design. I'm increasingly drawn toward UI design and motion — I find myself most engaged when work sits at the edge of visual craft and usability.

I work primarily in Figma and Blender, with some experience in HTML/CSS. I pick up tools quickly and I'm comfortable experimenting across different workflows — including integrating AI where it makes sense.

I'm grounded in UX process — user research, prototyping, usability testing — but my direction is moving toward the more visual and expressive end of design. I'm looking for roles where both craft and thinking are valued.

Outside of screens: classical guitar, sailing, reading, and staying active.
