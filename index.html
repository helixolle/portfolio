About
olle lomberg davegård

I'm a digital designer based in Gothenburg, with a background that runs through UI design, VFX, and 3D graphics. My strongest work sits at the intersection of visual craft and usability.

I work primarily in Figma. I have a working understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — enough to prototype in code and collaborate closely with developers. I pick up tools quickly and I'm comfortable integrating AI into my workflow where it makes sense.

I'm grounded in UX process — research, prototyping, interaction design — with a strong eye for the visual side of the craft.

Outside of screens: classical guitar, cooking, sailing, reading, and staying active
